# Warm

Warm earth tones — no cool colors — for human, lifestyle, and product writing.

## Colors

| Role | Hex | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Terracotta | `#D9603C` | Primary warm accent |
| Gold | `#E8B257` | Secondary warm accent |
| Orange | `#F08F4A` | Movement / energy |
| Cream | `#FFF6E8` | Base background |
| Deep ink | `#3D2A1F` | Line work, never pure black |

## Use Case

Lifestyle articles, brand storytelling, personal essays, emotional product writing. Also strong for covers needing human warmth.

## Pairs Well With

- Styles: `warm`, `watercolor`, `sketch-notes`, `editorial` (when warmth fits the story)
- Types: `scene`, `timeline`, `infographic` (lifestyle context)

## Anti-Pairs

- `blueprint`, `scientific` (color conflict — cool vs warm)
- `screen-print` with neon (palette fights itself)

## Generation Prompt Hints

Include: "warm earth tones — terracotta, gold, orange, cream — no cool colors, golden-hour mood, deep ink instead of pure black"
