# Cool

Cool blue / cyan / silver-white. The canonical tech / AI cover palette.

## Colors

| Role | Hex | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Deep blue | `#1E3A5F` | Anchor color |
| Cyan | `#4ABDD0` | Bright accent |
| Silver-white | `#E8EEF3` | Cool light |
| Ink | `#0F1A2C` | Deep dark for text |

## Use Case

Tech, AI, engineering, deep-tech feature covers. Conveys precision and future-orientation.

## Pairs Well With

- Styles: `blueprint`, `vector-illustration`, `editorial`, `flat`
- Types: cover, `framework`, `infographic`

## Anti-Pairs

- `warm` style (palette conflict)
- `nature`, `watercolor` (atmospheric clash)

## Generation Prompt Hints

Include: "cool blue and cyan palette with silver-white highlights on deep tech-blue base, precision tech-forward register"
