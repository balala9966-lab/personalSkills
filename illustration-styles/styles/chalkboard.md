# Chalkboard

Classroom chalkboard / handwritten explanation aesthetic.

## Visual Traits
- White/colored chalk on dark green or black
- Handwritten typography
- Sketchy diagrams with erasure marks
- Educational layout

## Best For
`infographic`, `flowchart`, `framework` — educational content where you want a "teacher explaining at the board" feel.

## Avoid For
Polished/formal contexts.

## Generation Prompt Hints
Include: "chalkboard illustration, white chalk on dark green, handwritten teacher style"
Avoid: "polished vector", "photorealistic"
