# Vintage

Aged parchment, historical feel — for history, heritage, story-of-X content.

## Visual Traits
- Aged paper / parchment background
- Faded ink, hand-drawn line work
- Historical motifs (banners, scrollwork, antique frames)
- Sepia / muted palette

## Best For
`scene`, `timeline` — historical narrative content.

## Avoid For
Anything contemporary or technical.

## Generation Prompt Hints
Include: "vintage aged parchment, faded ink, historical illustration, sepia tones"
Avoid: "modern", "vector", "neon"
