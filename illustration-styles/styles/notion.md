# Notion

Minimal hand-drawn line art on a neutral background — the "knowledge SaaS" register.

## Visual Traits

- **Line work**: thin lines with very slight wobble — controlled-organic
- **Fill**: mostly outline; occasional soft fill in 1-2 colors
- **Background**: off-white / neutral cream
- **Color discipline**: 2-3 colors max, kept soft
- **Typography**: clean sans labels, friendly
- **Composition**: airy, lots of whitespace, friendly icon-like shapes

## Best For

- `infographic`, `flowchart`, `framework`, `comparison`, `timeline` — extremely flexible
- SaaS docs, knowledge-sharing posts, tutorial-style writing where the reader should feel "this is helpful and approachable"

## Avoid For

- High-drama opinion pieces — too soft
- Photo-realism contexts

## Canonical Palette

Pairs best with: `macaron` for education, `warm` for product, default neutral for general SaaS.

## Generation Prompt Hints

Include:
- "minimal hand-drawn line art"
- "soft rounded icon-like shapes"
- "neutral background, plenty of whitespace"
- "friendly, approachable"

Avoid:
- "bold", "dramatic", "high-contrast" — break the soft character
- "photorealistic", "complex shading"
