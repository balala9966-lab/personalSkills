# Watercolor

Soft artistic watercolor washes. For atmospheric and emotional content where mood matters more than precision.

## Visual Traits

- **Line work**: soft edges, color bleed, no hard outlines
- **Fill**: layered translucent washes
- **Background**: paper-feel or soft gradient (note: avoid for editorial body if you want clean whitespace)
- **Color discipline**: harmonious analogous palettes; let colors bleed into each other
- **Typography**: rarely used; if present, hand-lettered
- **Composition**: organic, painterly, atmospheric

## Best For

- `scene` — primary use case
- `timeline` — dreamy growth journeys
- Lifestyle, travel, wellness, creative writing

## Avoid For

- `flowchart`, `framework` — the softness fights structure
- Technical content — too imprecise

## Canonical Palette

Pairs best with: harmonious natural palettes. `warm` for sunset moods, `earth` for nature, `pastel` for dreamy.

## Generation Prompt Hints

Include:
- "soft watercolor illustration"
- "painterly washes with color bleed"
- "atmospheric, dreamy mood"
- "loose organic edges"

Avoid:
- "hard edges", "vector", "flat"
- "technical diagram", "infographic"
- "high contrast"
