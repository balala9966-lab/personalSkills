# Vector Illustration

Clean flat vector art with bold shapes and high readability. The workhorse style for knowledge articles.

## Visual Traits

- **Line work**: solid, smooth, vector-like — not hand-drawn
- **Fill**: flat color blocks with clear edges
- **Background**: white or single subtle tint
- **Color discipline**: 3-5 colors max, each with a semantic role
- **Typography**: clean sans-serif labels, sparse
- **Composition**: clear visual hierarchy with icons and labels; subject 50-70% of frame

## Best For

- `infographic`, `flowchart`, `comparison`, `framework` — anywhere structure needs to read instantly
- Technical content that wants to feel modern and professional, not clinical

## Avoid For

- Narrative or emotional scenes — too clean to carry feeling
- Anything that should feel hand-touched or warm

## Canonical Palette

Pairs best with: any vivid clean palette. Use `macaron` for educational warmth, `cool` for tech, `warm` for product writing.

## Generation Prompt Hints

Include:
- "clean flat vector illustration"
- "bold geometric shapes"
- "minimal flat design"
- "white background, clear visual hierarchy"

Avoid:
- "hand-drawn", "sketch", "wobble" — these break the vector character
- "photorealistic", "3D rendered"
- "watercolor", "painterly"
