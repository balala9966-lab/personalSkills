# Flat

Modern bold geometric flat. Sharp angles, confident shapes.

## Visual Traits
- Bold flat shapes, no shading
- Strong color blocks, 3-4 colors
- White or light tint background
- Sans-serif typography

## Best For
`infographic`, `flowchart`, `comparison`, `framework` — modern contemporary content.

## Avoid For
Soft/emotional content.

## Generation Prompt Hints
Include: "modern flat design, bold geometric shapes, strong color blocks"
Avoid: "hand-drawn", "soft", "watercolor"
