# Pixel Art

Retro 8-bit game aesthetic — for game, retro-tech, or nostalgia content.

## Visual Traits
- Pixelated rendering
- Limited palette (game console style)
- Sprite-like characters and objects

## Best For
`scene`, gaming content, retro-tech writing.

## Avoid For
Most other contexts — pixel art is a strong register choice and breaks if applied casually.

## Generation Prompt Hints
Include: "8-bit pixel art, retro game sprite, limited palette, blocky rendering"
Avoid: "smooth", "vector", "photorealistic"
