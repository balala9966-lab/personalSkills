# Sketch Notes

Soft hand-drawn warm notes — the visual-notetaking aesthetic.

## Visual Traits

- **Line work**: hand-drawn with intentional wobble, varied weights
- **Fill**: occasional soft warm fills
- **Background**: light cream or pale macaron tint
- **Color discipline**: 3-4 macaron tones plus black/dark grey for line work
- **Typography**: handwritten labels integrated into illustration
- **Composition**: notebook-like, mixed elements (boxes, arrows, doodles), informal hierarchy

## Best For

- `infographic`, `flowchart`, `comparison`, `timeline` — the soft-structured workhorse
- Educational content where the reader should feel "this was taken by a thoughtful note-taker"

## Avoid For

- High-stakes formal contexts
- Pure narrative scenes — better styles exist

## Canonical Palette

Pairs best with: `macaron` (canonical). Also `warm` for warmer tone.

## Generation Prompt Hints

Include:
- "hand-drawn sketch notes style"
- "notebook aesthetic with handwritten labels"
- "soft macaron palette"
- "informal mixed elements: boxes, arrows, doodles"

Avoid:
- "polished vector"
- "photorealistic"
- "dark dramatic"
