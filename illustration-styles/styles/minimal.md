# Minimal

Ultra-clean, lots of whitespace, zen — when less truly is more.

## Visual Traits
- Single subject, minimal supporting elements
- 1-2 colors plus black
- Generous whitespace (>60% of frame)
- Subtle typography

## Best For
`infographic`, `comparison`, `framework` — single-concept visualization, philosophical content.

## Avoid For
Information-dense content.

## Generation Prompt Hints
Include: "ultra-minimal design, generous whitespace, single focal subject, zen aesthetic"
Avoid: "complex", "detailed", "busy"
