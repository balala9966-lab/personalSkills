# Sketch

Raw pencil-notebook feel — exploratory, draft-like.

## Visual Traits
- Pencil-like line work with sketch hatching
- No fill or rough shading
- Paper-feel or off-white background
- Handwritten annotations

## Best For
`scene`, brainstorming-style content, "thinking out loud" pieces.

## Avoid For
Polished final-product feel.

## Generation Prompt Hints
Include: "rough pencil sketch on notebook paper, hatching, handwritten notes, exploratory"
Avoid: "polished", "vector", "clean"
