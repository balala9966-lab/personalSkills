# Warm

Friendly, approachable, soft palette. For when the article wants to feel like a conversation with a friend.

## Visual Traits

- **Line work**: hand-drawn with gentle curves
- **Fill**: soft warm fills with light texture
- **Background**: cream / soft peach / pale gold
- **Color discipline**: warm earth tones — terracotta, gold, ochre, soft orange; no cool colors
- **Typography**: handwritten or rounded sans, friendly
- **Composition**: cozy, slightly off-center, golden-hour mood

## Best For

- `scene` — primary use case; carries narrative warmth
- `timeline` — when the timeline is personal (growth, journey)
- Personal essays, reflections, lifestyle, brand storytelling

## Avoid For

- `framework`, `blueprint`-style content — too soft
- Technical or data-driven pieces

## Canonical Palette

Pairs best with: `warm` (this is the canonical match). Also works with `earth` for nature-tinted content.

## Generation Prompt Hints

Include:
- "warm hand-drawn illustration"
- "friendly approachable feel"
- "golden hour lighting, soft warm palette"
- "cozy atmosphere"
- "terracotta, gold, soft orange tones"

Avoid:
- "cool colors", "blue", "neon"
- "technical", "schematic", "blueprint"
