# Elegant

Refined, polished, editorial-grade — the look of a high-end magazine or thought-leadership piece.

## Visual Traits
- Refined typography (often serif or refined sans)
- Subtle gold/deep green/cream palette
- Generous whitespace
- Sophisticated composition

## Best For
`infographic`, `comparison`, `framework`, `timeline` — business, thought leadership, historical content.

## Avoid For
Casual or playful content.

## Generation Prompt Hints
Include: "elegant editorial illustration, refined typography, sophisticated palette, polished"
Avoid: "casual", "doodle", "playful"
